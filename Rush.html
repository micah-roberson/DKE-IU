Rush Delta Kappa Epsilon

Welcome to our Rush program! We're excited to introduce you to the Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity and provide you with an opportunity to become a part of our brotherhood.

What is Rush?

Rush is a chance for prospective members to get to know our fraternity, meet our current brothers, and learn about our values and activities.

Why Join Delta Kappa Epsilon?

Joining Delta Kappa Epsilon means joining a lifelong brotherhood that values leadership, camaraderie, personal growth, and community service.
