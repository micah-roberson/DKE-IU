Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Delta Kappa Epsilon (DKE)?
Delta Kappa Epsilon, often referred to as “Deke”, is a prominent and historic fraternity founded on principles of brotherhood, leadership, and community impact. With chapters across various campuses, DKE is committed to fostering personal growth, meaningful connections, and positive change.
How can I join DKE?
If you're interested in joining DKE, we encourage you to reach out to our recruitment chairman, Dalijah Marks (ddmarks@iu.edu), or our general email (DKE.DeltaPsi.IU@gmail.com), for information about the recruitment process, membership requirements, and upcoming events.
Is diversity and equity important to DKE?
Absolutely. DKE is committed to creating an inclusive environment that values individuals from all backgrounds. We celebrate different perspectives and work towards fostering a culture of respect and unity.
What events and activities does DKE offer?
DKE chapters host a variety of events and activities, including brotherhood gatherings, community service initiatives, leadership workshops, social events, and philanthropic campaigns. These activities provide opportunities for personal growth, networking, and making a positive impact.
What opportunities for leadership are available in DKE?
DKE offers various leadership roles within the chapter, ranging from executive board positions to committee chairs. These roles provide hands-on experience in areas such as organization, management, event planning, and more.
What benefits does DKE offer for personal development?
Joining DKE can lead to personal growth in numerous ways. From building lifelong friendships to enhancing leadership skills, honing time management, and developing a strong sense of responsibility, DKE provides a supportive environment for individual development.
How can I stay connected with DKE after graduation?
DKE's strong alumni network offers opportunities to stay connected with your fraternity brothers, mentor current members, and contribute to the ongoing success of DKE chapters. Alumni often engage in networking events, chapter support, and mentoring initiatives.
What is the history of DKE?
DKE was founded in 1844 at Yale University and has a rich legacy of producing notable alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields. The fraternity has a longstanding tradition of promoting brotherhood, leadership, and community involvement.
How can I get in touch with DKE?
For inquiries or more information, please feel free to reach out to us at DKE.DeltaPsi.IU@Gmail.com or fill out our contact form. We'd be happy to assist you!

We hope these FAQs have provided you with valuable information about Delta Kappa Epsilon. If you have any more questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. We're excited to welcome you into the DKE community!
